URC Rover

Robotics systems work involving autonomy, perception, communications, and manipulation for a competition rover platform.

Project Overview

The URC rover combines autonomous navigation, sensing, remote operation, subsystem integration, and arm manipulation in a single multi-team robotics effort.

This is one of the strongest examples of large-scale systems integration in my portfolio.

Focus Areas

  • ROS2 integration
  • Camera and LiDAR-based perception
  • Telemetry and constrained communications
  • Manipulator kinematics and control

My Role

My work has included computational/robotics leadership, autonomy organization, perception pipeline coordination, and communications integration between the rover and base station.
