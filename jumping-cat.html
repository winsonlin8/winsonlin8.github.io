Jumping Cat Robot

A biomimetic jumping quadruped project focused on motor control, electronics integration, and physically grounded robotic behavior.

Project Overview

This project centers on a cat-inspired robot capable of dynamic jumping behavior. It combines sensing, motor control, power distribution, embedded control logic, and simulation-guided behavior development.

Focus Areas

  • Motor control
  • Embedded safety limits
  • Power distribution
  • Reinforcement learning simulation

My Role

I have worked on the electrical and computational side of the project, including integrated sensing, motor control, power electronics, safety constraints, and simulation support for jump/gait development.
