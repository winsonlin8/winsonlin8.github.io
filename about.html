About Me

I study engineering with a focus on building real systems across hardware and software.

I am an undergraduate at Rice University studying Electrical and Computer Engineering, with a specialization in Neuroengineering and minors in Neuroscience, Engineering Design, and Asian Studies.

My work includes robotics, embedded systems, wireless communication, sensing, and interdisciplinary engineering design. I am especially interested in applications in robotics, medical devices, and interactive systems.

I enjoy projects that require system integration across mechanics, electronics, firmware, software, and user-facing design.
