Projects

A few projects spanning research, robotics, and embedded systems.

Wi-Fi HaLow Telemetry for Neurotechnology

Investigated the feasibility of IEEE 802.11ah for low-power telemetry in neurotechnology systems, including throughput, latency, and energy characterization.

Focus areas: wireless systems, power measurement, experimental design, and system tradeoffs.

URC Rover

Contributed to a rover platform involving ROS2 integration, vision systems, communication pipelines, and subsystem coordination across software, electrical, and mechanical teams.

Focus areas: robotics integration, perception, remote operation, and team leadership.

Embedded Systems and Prototyping

Worked on firmware, wireless modules, PCB-related tasks, debugging, and lab test infrastructure for research and engineering projects.

Focus areas: microcontrollers, debugging, measurement, and hardware-software integration.
